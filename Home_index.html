Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu's architectural greatness lies in its stunning Dravidian temples adorned with towering gopurams, intricate carvings, and vibrant sculptures. These temples, such as the Brihadeeswarar Temple and Meenakshi Temple, are not just architectural wonders but also cultural and spiritual landmarks attracting visitors worldwide. From opulent palaces to functional water management systems like temple tanks and stepwells, the state's architecture blends beauty with utility, leaving an indelible mark on its landscape and culture.

Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh's architectural greatness shines through its intricate temples, palaces, and forts, each a testament to the region's rich cultural heritage. Dravidian and Vijayanagara architectural styles dominate, with iconic structures like the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple and the grand Chandragiri Fort showcasing the state's architectural splendor.

Karnataka

Karnataka's architectural brilliance is epitomized by its awe-inspiring temples, majestic palaces, and intricate monuments. From the grandeur of the Mysore Palace to the UNESCO-listed ruins of Hampi, Karnataka's diverse architectural heritage reflects centuries of cultural richness and artistic mastery.

Orissa

Odisha's architectural greatness is embodied in its magnificent temples, characterized by their intricate carvings, towering spires, and spiritual significance. From the iconic Sun Temple in Konark to the majestic Jagannath Temple in Puri, Odisha's architecture reflects the rich cultural heritage and devotion of its people.
